Hello, I'm Tara Jayne

✦

I help you find your way back home to your body. We're often told to "just let it go." But when you've lived through trauma — whether one defining moment or years of carrying too much — your body doesn't simply move on. You might feel disconnected, like you're living from the neck up. A tight chest that never quite softens. Shoulders that stay lifted, always bracing for something that never comes. I know this because I've lived it.

I'm Tara Jayne, an Intuitive Massage Therapist and Reiki Healer. My work isn't just about relaxation — it's about reclamation. I realised early on that standard massage wasn't enough for the layers many of us carry. Trauma isn't only held in the mind; it settles into the fascia, the nervous system, and the energetic body. By blending intuitive massage with Reiki, I support your body to gently un-armour at its own pace.

I come from a life shaped by fire, loss, resilience and the kind of transformation that only happens when everything familiar falls away. My path has taken me through addiction, trauma, healing, and the slow rebuilding of a self I thought I had lost. Every lesson, every scar and every breakthrough has become part of the work I now bring to the world.

Restore and Align was born from that journey. It is a space where the body, the soul and the conscious self are welcomed back into harmony. Through intuitive massage, Reiki, crystal healing, breathwork and embodied guidance, I support others in restoring what life has taken from them and aligning with the truth they forgot they carried.

My skills are not just qualifications. They are lived experiences held with compassion, grounded presence and a deep understanding of what it means to rise when you thought you couldn't. I offer healing for the body, empowerment for the spirit and a gentle, steady hand for anyone ready to reclaim themselves. This work isn't just a service. It is a calling to help the collective return to balance, peace and self-connection, one person at a time.
